our mission.

As a small business owner you may have found yourself struggling to figure out the right place to run your small business. We don't blame you! At Hop Shop, our goal is to assist you as you navigate your journey as an entrepreneur.

We know how scary it can be to face the issues of competing against other small businesses, learning brand yourself effectively, being unsure of your business decisions, and tracking your progress in over-all growth. These circumstances are distracting and can make it difficult for your business to thrive or be financially sustainable in the long-term.

We're going to help you connect to the large community of passionate creators and supporters. We'll provide you with the tips and insights you need to grow along the way. We know that running a small business can be extremely nervewrecking, especially when you don't have any guidance or mentorship. If you're interested in running your business with Hop Shop, sign up to join the waiting list. We hope to assist you in the near future!
